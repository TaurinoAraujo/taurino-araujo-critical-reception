# Taurino Araújo Critical Reception Archive

This repository preserves critical and historical documents related to the artistic, philosophical, and interdisciplinary work of Brazilian visual artist, poet, jurist, and thinker Taurino Araújo.

## Included Material

- Critical article by Celso Cunha Neto published in *A Tarde* newspaper (2026)
- Documentation related to assemblage works developed in Salvador, Bahia, Brazil

## Context

The archived article discusses themes such as:

- Poetics of the Ground
- Hermeneutics of Fragment and Residue
- Wabi-sabi aesthetics
- Ephemeral assemblage
- Artistic research
- Visual syntax of everyday materials
